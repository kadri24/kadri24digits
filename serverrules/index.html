Community Guidelines

1. Behavior & Respect

  • Treat all members with respect. Harassment, bullying, hate speech, or discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated.
  • No personal attacks, insults, or inflammatory behavior toward others.
  • Avoid excessive arguing, drama, or behavior that disrupts server harmony.

2. Content Guidelines

  • No NSFW or inappropriate content, including text, images, links, usernames, or profile pictures.
  • No gore, graphic violence, disturbing imagery, or content intended to shock others.
  • No discussion of illegal activities, distribution of illegal content, or piracy.
  • Keep content and discussions appropriate for a general audience. Remember that users of all ages may be present.

3. Chat Rules

  • Keep conversations in the appropriate channels. Check channel descriptions before posting.
  • No spamming, excessive emojis, or repeated messages. This includes text walls, excessive caps, or image spam.
  • No self-promotion, advertisements, or unsolicited links without permission from the moderators.
  • English is the primary language in public channels to ensure moderation effectiveness.
Moderation Policy

4. Interactions with Moderation

  • Respect the decisions of moderators and staff. If you disagree with a moderation action, you're able to appeal through the proper channels.
  • Do not impersonate staff members or pretend to have moderation abilities.
  • Report rule violations to moderators instead of confronting users publicly.

5. Voice Chat Etiquette

  • No disruptive sounds, music, or voice changers that interfere with conversation.
  • Respect others who are speaking and avoid talking over people.
  • Do not join voice channels just to be disruptive or to harass others.

6. Additional Guidelines

  • Keep politics, religion, and other potentially divisive topics to a minimum.
  • No discussion about account sharing, exploits, hacks, or cheats in our games.
  • Follow Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines at all times.
Privacy & Safety

7. Personal Information

  • Do not share personal information about yourself or others (addresses, phone numbers, etc.).
  • No doxxing or threats to reveal private information about others.
  • Be careful when sharing social media accounts or other contact information.
