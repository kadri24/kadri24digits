Our Games

Eyes - The Horror Game

You break into a haunted mansion at night. You must find and steal bags of money to escape, but a monster named Krasue is also there searching for you. Look for eye runes on the walls and use them to watch through her eyes. Will you be able to find all the bags and escape alive?

Eyes - Scary Thriller

A more redefined version of Eyes the Horror Game is here! Steal every bag of money from the mansion, the hospital and the school. Use the eye runes to see through the Krasue's vision. Will you escape or become her next victim?

Slendrina The Cellar

The third installment of the Slendrina series is finally here! After escaping the hospital you must now escape from the cellar. Find every key to unlock every door and try to survive the darkness. But beware, she is watching everywhere...

Slender: The Eight Pages

The classic horror game that started it all! Find the eight pages hidden in the forest before the Slender Man catches you. Use your flashlight to navigate through the darkness, but be careful not to stay in one place for too long. Can you collect all the pages and escape?
